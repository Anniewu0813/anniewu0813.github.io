Research Interest

Finite Field Arithmetic Operations and Applications

This direction of research focuses on investigating efficient algorithms and novel architectures for the computation of finite field operations according to their practical applications.

Intelligent System Security and Fault Detection

These systems are often referred to feedback control systems that use communication networks for information exchange among various system-units such as sensors, and controllers.

VLSI Digital Signal/Image Processing Systems

This research focuses on developing novel techniques to map advanced digital signal/image algorithms to architectures in various platforms, including FPGA-based image registration, pattern recognition, and machine learning systems.

Hardware Security

This direction of research focuses on developing novel techniques to protect hardware IPs in both ASIC and FPGA platforms.
